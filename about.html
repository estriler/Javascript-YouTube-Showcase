About the Animator




Hello! My name is Eric Striler and I'm an animator and aspiring web developer.

I graduated Webster University in 2011 with a BA in animation. For the last few years, I've been working hard building my brand as an animator and cranking out commissions for some big-name YouTubers on the side.

Now, I'm shifting gears to add software development to my toolbelt - with an eye for design and quality control, my specialty is Front-End Web Development but I'm always adding new languages, frameworks, and tools to my stack. If you're in need of a skilled developer with a strong background in design, a keen eye for detail, and a creative mind, please contact me.
